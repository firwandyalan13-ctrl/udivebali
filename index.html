UDive Bali: The Gold Standard in Tulamben

Since 2018, UDive has been rooted in Tulamben, Bali — home to shipwrecks, macro life, and dramatic drop-offs. It's a diver's pilgrimage site and an instructor's paradise.

  • Avg. 8,000+ dives per instructor: Our team knows these waters inside and out.
  • Avg. 700+ students taught per instructor: They've seen every type of nervous beginner — and have the patience and experience to match.
  • All instructors are trained directly by UDive's own instructor programme.
  • Address: Tulamben, Kubu, Karangasem Regency, Bali

Teaching Philosophy: Safety First, Quality Always

At UDive, we refuse to run a conveyor-belt certification factory. What we truly care about is whether every breath you take underwater feels completely natural.

🎓 Small Group Classes Max 4 students per class — 100% instructor attention for every student
🐟 Neutral Buoyancy Core We drill buoyancy from day one, so you move underwater like a fish — not a bulldozer
🌿 Eco-First Mindset Learn to dive and learn to protect the ocean — become a true guardian of the blue
🎯 Personalised Teaching Whether you're a natural in water or terrified of it, we'll find your pace together
